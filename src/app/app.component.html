    Observable

    • Declarative
    • Unicast (each subscriber gets its own execution)
    • Immutable
    • Lazily executed (only when subscribed)

    Subject

    • Imperative
    • Multicast (it maintains a list of subscribers and notifies them when the value is emmitted.)
    • Mutable
    • Eagerly executed (when events occur)
